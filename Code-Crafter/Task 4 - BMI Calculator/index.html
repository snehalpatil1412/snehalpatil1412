Diet Plans

Underweight Diet Plan

Eat calorie-rich, nutrient-dense foods such as nuts, seeds, and whole-fat dairy. Increase meal frequency and add healthy fats to your diet.

Normal Weight Diet Plan

Maintain a balanced diet with a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Ensure portion control and regular exercise.

Overweight Diet Plan

Focus on reducing portion sizes, increasing vegetable and fruit intake, limiting processed foods, and engaging in regular physical activity.
